Design System · Shared Principles

These apply to whichever palette you pick — they define the personality of the app regardless of color.

Typography
Morning Loop
8.4 km · 1:42
SF Pro Rounded throughout — keeps the friendly outdoor feel without going childish. Heavy weights for stats, medium for labels.
Shape Language
Capsule
Card
✓
Capsule buttons for actions. Generous corner radii (12–20px) on cards. Circular icons and dots. No sharp rectangles.
Material / Depth
Frosted glass overlay
.thinMaterial / .regularMaterial
System materials for overlays — they adapt to light/dark and look native. Avoid custom semi-transparent flat colors.
Map Style
Whichever tile style complements the chosen palette:
Liberty (current)
Warm, detailed, hand-drawn feel
Positron / Bright
Minimal, high-contrast, clean
Recommendation: Direction 1 (Field Journal) feels most distinctly Sapling — warm, personal, and tactile. Direction 2 is the safer, more modern choice. Direction 3 is bolder and more expressive. The structure of all three is the same — only the palette needs to be decided before any code changes.
